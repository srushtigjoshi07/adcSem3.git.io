Problem Statement

The audio output from devices like laptops and mobile phones is insufficient to directly drive a speaker. This project focuses on designing an LM386-based audio amplifier to amplify weak audio signals and deliver audible sound through a speaker system.

Challenge:

Consumer electronic devices typically output low-power audio signals that require amplification for practical speaker use.

2

Objectives

  • To design an LM386 audio amplifier circuit capable of amplifying low-level audio signals
  • To amplify weak audio signals from portable devices effectively
  • To drive an 8-ohm speaker efficiently with clear audio output
3

Methodology / Approach

The project follows a systematic approach to audio signal amplification:

1
Audio Input

Audio signal is provided from a laptop through a 3.5mm audio jack

2
Signal Coupling

Signal passes through a coupling capacitor for DC blocking

3
Volume Control

10kΩ potentiometer allows adjustment of input signal level

4
Amplification

LM386 IC amplifies the signal and delivers it to the speaker
